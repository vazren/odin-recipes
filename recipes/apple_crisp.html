Apple Crisp

Description

This apple crisp is incredibly easy to make and will have the whole household wanting more! The ingredients are simple and common. This dish will be the star of any gathering!

Ingredients

  • 6-8 apples, cleaned and quartered
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • Lemon juice to keep apples looking fresh

Steps

  1. Place apples in buttered pan. Add lemon juice if they start to brown
  2. Work together flour, butter, cinnamon and sugar with fingers or pastry blender until crumbly
  3. Pack closely over apples. If apples get dry add water to bottom of pan
  4. Bake at 375 for 45-60 minutes. Test apples with fork to check tenderness
