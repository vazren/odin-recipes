Chicken Vesuvio

Description

The incredible chicken vesuvio dish is a classic, made with chicken breast, red potatoes and peas. This combination provides the perfect combination of elements to create a delicious dish for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 6-8 oz boneless, skinless, trimmed and pounded chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper
  • 5 tsp olive oil
  • 12 oz red potatoes, unpeeled and cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 1 minced garlic clove
  • 1/4 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/8 tsp dried rosemary
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas, thawed
  • 2 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Steps

  1. Pat chicken dry and dredge in shallow dish of flour
  2. Heat 1 Tbsp olive oil in nonstick skillet over medium high heat and brown chicken 3-4 minutes per side, then transfer to plate and wipe skillet clean
  3. Heat remaining oil over medium high heat until shimmering. Add potatoes and cook, stirring occasionally for 7 minutes
  4. Stir in garlic, oregano, rosemary, and 1/8 tsp of salt and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Stir in broth and wine and scrape up browned bits
  5. Nestle chicken and juices into potatoes and bring to simmer. Reduce heat to medium low and simmer until chicken registers 160 degrees, 12-18 minutes, flipping chicken halfway through
  6. Transfer chicken to plate and tent loosely with foil
  7. Increase heat to medium and continue to simmer potatoes and sauce until potatoes are tender and sauce is thickened, 5-7 minutes
  8. Transfer potatoes to platter with chicken and add peas, butter and lemon juice to pan off heat
  9. Combine all elements on platter and serve
