Spanish Rice

Description

This is a family recipe passed down through generations to make the most delicious Spanish rice imaginable. The ingredients are simple and easily found at your local grocery store. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 lb Kielbasa sausage
  • 5 cloves chopped garlic
  • 1/2 tsp Montreal grillmates
  • 1/2 jar Sofrito
  • 1 can pigeon peas
  • 1 Tbsp Adobo seasoning
  • 2 lbs white rice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 Sazon packets
  • 4 pigeon pea cans water

Steps

  1. Place large pot on stove and set temp to medium high
  2. Add cooking oil and salt to pot
  3. Chop sausage into 1/2 inch pieces and add to pot
  4. Add garlic and Montreal grillmates to pot
  5. Mix together and let fry on bottom for approx 2 minutes
  6. Add Sofrito to pot and stir together will
  7. Remove from heat
  8. Add pigeon peas and Sazon packets; mix together
  9. Add rice and turn to coat rice until rice is a red orange color
  10. Pour water into pot and add Adobo seasoning
  11. Place back on high heat and let boil
  12. After boiling begins, turn heat down to medium low. Place lid on pot allowing for slight ventilation
  13. Set timer for 25-30 minutes
  14. Turn rice when water is absorbed
